03 · the count

The "eleven generations" is folklore.

Anchored to a documented birth around 1604, run forward to a descendant born today. The arithmetic doesn't fit.

~422 years ÷ 10 intervals = ~40 yrs / generation

Families don't average forty-year generations across four centuries. The line is closer to twelve generations than eleven. A person is missing — and we can locate the slot.
