How to finish it

In the file dialog that opened, navigate to wherever your brand font files live and select them. These are usually .woff2, .woff, .ttf, or .otf files. Based on your design system, you'll want to grab the fonts for your Display & Headings style and your Body Serif style (your deck headings use a bold sans, and the heritage site uses a serif — so you likely have at least two font families to upload). Select them all and confirm in the dialog.

Once they upload, the "Missing brand fonts" warning should clear, and both your slide deck and heritage site previews will re-render with the real typography instead of the substitutes.
